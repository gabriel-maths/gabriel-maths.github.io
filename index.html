Simon Gabriel's homepage

I am a PostDoc in the Mathematics Cluster of Excellence at the University of Münster, in the research group of Hendrik Weber. My interests lie in stochastic analysis and Markov processes, in particular stochastic partial differential equations and interacting particle systems.

Before coming to Münster, I was a PhD student at the University of Warwick, advised by Nikos Zygouras and Paul Chleboun. I completed both my BSc and MSc in Maths at the Technical University of Munich.

You can contact me via email: simon.gabriel@uni-muenster.de
